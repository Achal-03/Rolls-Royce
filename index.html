About Rolls-Royce

Rolls-Royce Motor Cars is a British luxury automobile maker. A wholly owned subsidiary of BMW AG, it is based in Goodwood, England. The company’s cars are synonymous with excellence, luxury, and a unique blend of engineering and artistry.

Every Rolls-Royce is hand-built to order, with each detail tailored to the owner’s desires. The result is not just a car, but a masterpiece.
